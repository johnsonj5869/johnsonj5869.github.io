Dr. Joshua (Josh) Johnson

Chiropractic Physician

Dr. Joshua Johnson is a chiropractic physician with a strong foundation in movement science, nutrition, and evidence-based care. At Aligned Modern Health, he guides patients through functional recovery using spinal adjustments, targeted rehabilitation, and therapeutic exercise—all grounded in the belief that long-term strength and independence are key to lasting relief.

Dr. Johnson's approach emphasizes education and empowerment, helping patients understand their bodies, improve mobility, and know when to seek care. He is trained in the McKenzie Method, DNS, and various soft tissue and adjusting techniques, with a special interest in restoring movement patterns for chronic pain and postural strain.

With academic distinction in both chiropractic and nutrition, Dr. Johnson brings a whole-person perspective to musculoskeletal care, supporting individuals who want to feel better, move better, and stay better.

Specializations

  • Lower back pain
  • Individuals with chronic pain
  • McKenzie Method for spinal and joint pain
  • Functional rehabilitation and corrective exercise
  • Evidence-based treatment planning
  • Office workers and desk-bound professionals

Education & Clinical Credentials

  • Doctor of Chiropractic, Palmer College of Chiropractic
  • B.S. in Biobehavioral Health and Nutritional Sciences, Schreyer Honors College at Penn State
  • Certified in McKenzie Method Part A and Thompson Technique
  • Training in DNS, Flexion-Distraction, and extremity adjusting techniques
